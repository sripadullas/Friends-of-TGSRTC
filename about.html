About Friends of RTC

Friends of RTC is a volunteer-driven community that supports safer, cleaner, and more accessible bus transport. We organise workshops, drive cleanliness campaigns, and partner with transit authorities to improve passenger experience.

Our mission

Promote safety, accessibility, and community participation.

Our vision

Visible public transport that is safe, reliable, and respectful to all passengers.
